5. Give Reasons (Condensed)

  • Phytoplankton are called producers because they perform photosynthesis, forming the base of aquatic food chains.
  • Fungi are known as decomposers because they secrete enzymes to break down dead organic matter and recycle nutrients.
  • Eagles are defined as top consumers because they occupy the highest trophic level with no natural predators.
  • All components have equal importance for a balanced ecosystem because biotic and abiotic factors are interdependent; disrupting one affects the whole system.
  • Ecosystem is beneficial to humans as it provides vital services like oxygen production, water purification, and food/raw materials.
  • Eco-friendly development is called sustainable because it meets present needs without harming the environment or future resources.
