Part B: Internal Structure of Gills

i) Label a, b, c and f.

  • a: Basidiospore
  • b: Basidium
  • c: Paraphyses
  • f: Hyphae / Tramal tissue

ii) Role of d and f.

  • Role of d (Hymenium): Site of meiosis and production of basidiospores.
  • Role of f (Trama / Hyphae): Provides structural support and transports nutrients.

iii) How are basidiospores produced? (Short)

  1. Karyogamy: Haploid nuclei fuse to form diploid nucleus.
  2. Meiosis: Produces four haploid nuclei inside basidium.
  3. Spore Formation: Each nucleus enters a sterigma and forms a basidiospore.
